Warwickshire Based Counselling

Welcome to MindWell Counselling. I’m an experienced person-centred counsellor based in Southam, Warwickshire providing counselling for adults and young people experiencing depression and anxiety.
I currently have availability for new clients for in-person or online, also for creative therapy and walk and talk therapy.

Initial Consultation

I understand that contacting a counsellor to talk about your thoughts and feelings can seem daunting or overwhelming.
That’s why, from your first phone call or email, I take time to support you and to answer any questions you may have about counselling or the way I work. 

Please use the menu to navigate my website which will answer any questions you may have about the counselling process.
